The Heart of Our Farm: Who We Are and What We Stand For


Photo by Wirestock on freepik.com

Nestled in the heart of Elberta, Alabama, Farm to Table CSA is more than a farm; it's a commitment to our community and the environment. What began as a small family-run endeavor with a passion for organic farming has flourished into a trusted source of fresh, seasonal produce. From planting to harvest, we use USDA-certified organic, non-GMO seeds and heirloom plants to ensure our produce is as wholesome as it is flavorful.

At Farm to Table CSA, sustainability is not just a goal; it is our way of life. By prioritizing local agriculture, we eliminate the middleman, reduce food miles, and deliver the freshest harvest straight to your table.

We operate under the Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) model, fostering a direct connection between our farm and the families we serve. Members of our CSA enjoy weekly shares of our harvest during the vibrant fall and spring growing seasons. This partnership is not just about produce; it is about building a community rooted in trust, health, and sustainability.
