How does a CSA Work?

Farm to Table offers 8-week CSA shares two times per year, in the fall and in the spring. Shares are delivered weekly to our customers during the growing seasons. Delivery is free within a 30-mile radius of Elberta, Alabama. Pick-up is available for those outside the delivery area.
